About ezEML

 

ezEML is a web-based application designed to streamline the creation of metadata in the Ecological Metadata Language (EML).

The EML standard was designed to handle a wide variety of data scenarios. As a result, EML is complex and the learning curve to generating EML can be steep. Many data scenarios require only a relatively small subset of EML, however, and in such scenarios ezEML can greatly simplify the process of creating EML, especially for users who are new to EML or use it only infrequently.

ezEML was created by the Environmental Data Initiative (EDI).

Our goal with ezEML is to provide an easy-to-use, do-it-yourself tool for creating EML in relatively simple data scenarios. We hope you find it useful, and we welcome your feedback.
